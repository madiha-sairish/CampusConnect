About this event

Join students from every major for a full weekend of building, learning, and shipping real projects. You will form small teams, pick a challenge theme (AI for good, campus life, or open innovation), and present a working demo to a panel of faculty and industry mentors.

No experience required — we run onboarding blocks for Git, Figma, and APIs on Friday night. Meals, caffeine, and overnight lab access are included so you can focus on your build. Come for the skills, stay for the community.

Event highlights

  • Guest speakers from two alumni-led startups (Sat morning)
  • Free food, snacks, and midnight pizza runs
  • Networking lounge with recruiters and club leads
  • Certificates for all finishers · cash prizes for top three teams
